Definition


This Machine is designed to place the bottle by semi-manual bottles in and out only. And, automatically the bottles start rotating and nozzles up and down after that PC bottle is placed to washing part. The nozzle are moved up and down in the bottle, and spray rinsing water at a high pressure of 80bar. Generally, PC bottle is easily contaminated with green algae and scale. And, also, sometime itâ€™s contaminated with edible oil which is difficult to wash with existing washing machine. Our High Pressure Washing Machine can remove those of all contaminations up to 99.9%. Washing Capacity is 200bph for 2 side washing per once. It can be possible to wash the external of bottle as an option.
